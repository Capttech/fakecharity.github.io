Star Trek Online

Have you ever wanted to captain your own ship?

Ship VS Ship Battles

Ship battles are put together really well. You can put together your own ship in the way you want to. If you want a large curser with very strong shields or a small fast ship you can build it either way. Finally you can any weapons you want as well, anything from phasers to photon torpedoes to cannons.

Graphics

Star trek online graphics are pretty good. The graphics can relate the same graphics as star wars online. I personally love the ship graphics over the rest of the game. The ship 3D models are very detailed and they have full 360 degree textures.
